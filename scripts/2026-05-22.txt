A quick fact to kick things off: today is National Maritime Day in the United States. On this date in 1819, the steamship Savannah became the first vessel to cross the Atlantic with the help of steam power — completing the voyage from Georgia to Liverpool in 29 days. She was mostly under sail, but that engine made the history books. Global shipping has not stopped reinventing itself since.
